Introduction

Hi. My name is Andreea. I am a 16-year-old teenager. Since I was little, I dreamed of having my own brand of clothes, perfumes, bags, and many other things. And so, I managed to fulfill my dream as a small child, at 16 years old. It was not easy. It was a long and difficult road, with many obstacles and difficulties, with many nerves and problems. But also with many beautiful and unforgettable moments. Even if I'm just at the beginning of the road, I'm sure that through a lot of work, effort, and dedication I will succeed in making my dream the most beautiful reality. But until then, I present you my own brand:

LOUIS VUITTON

Louis Vuitton is a Moldovan luxury fashion house and company founded in 2023 by Zubic Andreea.
